About

CM College of Arts and Science, Nadavayal, Wayanad, occupies a prestigious position in the educational, socio-cultural sphere of Wayanad district. The college conducts seven UG programmes and three PG programmes. The college has an excellent academic reputation and conducts various programmes for the students of all departments.

The college conducts a career workshop and a mega placement drive named Prayukti on every year to enhance the employment opportunities of the qualified youths across Kerala. The eminent public speakers of our society address a wide range of topics in the educational system.
